## Executive Summary

A fully automated SEO content site system that generates revenue through **crypto-affiliate programs and non-custodial crypto payments** exclusively (designed for users in sanctioned regions where Amazon Associates, Google AdSense, PayPal, and Stripe are blocked).

### Revenue Streams (All Crypto-Payable)

1. **Exchange Referral Commissions** — MEXC (up to 70%), Bybit (40%). Every referred user's trading fees generate ongoing commission for life.
2. **Crypto Payment Processors** — BTCPay Server (self-hosted, zero-fee), NOWPayments (merchant gateway, 0.5% fee), CoinGate (back-end option). Use to accept payments on your content site.
3. **Display Advertising** — Adsterra ($5 min payout in crypto) and PropellerAds for publishers in restricted regions. Alternative to Google AdSense which won't serve Iranian content creators.
4. **Sponsored Content** — Crypto companies and SaaS tools actively sponsor reviews; they pay directly in USDT/BTC.
